Basic Knowlage:

Swift is a Programming Language made for Developing MacOS, IOS, WatchOS, or IPadOS Applications

The Development of Swift started in 2010 and was inspired by Objective-C, C#, Rust, Haskel, Ruby, Python, and many other languages. In June 4th of 2014 Swift was unveiled at WWDC (World Wide Developer Confrence) 2014 and the first application made using Swift was WWDC. After the confrence Registered Apple Developers got the beta to swift. They also released a book to go with it Called "The Swift Programming Language" And it's still being updated to the latest Info.

Swift 1.0 was released in September 9th of 2014 along with Xcode 6.0. In 2015 Swift 2.0 was released allowing Developers to publish apps on the apple appstore. Swift 3.0 was released in September 15th 2016, then swift 4.0 was released,
